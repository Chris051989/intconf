About the summit
______

Global value creation, cross-border innovation and global HR are some topics that define the increasingly complex challenges of leading a business in todays world. To meet these, corporations and their leaders need to develop the willingness to cooperate, the capacity to define globally shared values and the ability to formulate cultural commonalities.
Herein the concept of transculturality which, at its most basic, emphasizes the recognition and relation of cultural differences and similarities, will contribute to the development of industry-wide solutions, daily practice of organizations and the development of globally leading executives.

TLS is a summit designed for both the conceptualization of new challenges of leadership, and the interdisciplinary exchange of leading executives from diverse backgrounds.

The Transcultural Leadership Summit represents an information platform to deepen participants’ cultural knowledge and foster mutual understanding with its unique feature of an annually changing country focus. - This year the country in focus is China.

Why China?
______

Within the past months the major decrease in Chinese GDP has been marking a watershed in the seemingly unlimited emerge of Chinese Economy. In the course of the 21st century previous guarantors of Chinese high speed development like large-scale investments seem no longer sufficient to guarantee for constant and sustainable growth.
For China the present change comes with the opportunity to reshape their position in the world and an increasing need for alternative solutions, making it a fit and highly relevant topic for this years TLS.

Schedule

Location

Located in the context of Zeppelin University which has been ranked among the best and most innovative universities in Germany for its approach of “bridging business culture politics” the summits most unique asset becomes clear.
